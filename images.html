This utility has been developed to rate the quality of CBF maps with the goal of developing an automated quality evaluation index.

Instructions

1. Please enter your email and name and click on Next to start the review process.

2. For each image, please rate the CBF maps on a scale between 1 and 4:

1: Unacceptable

2: Poor

3: Average

4: Excellent

3. Click next in the image panel and the rating panel. Note, you need to click on both the panels; please make sure the image names match
